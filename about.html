About Me

Where I'm from.

I was born and raised in Fresno, CA. I've only ever lived in California so, it would be nice to try a new state someday.

Where I've been and where I'm going.

I come from a mostly artistic background. I wanted to be a painter but didn't like the idea of starving so I worked retail for many years before becoming a professional seamstress. My speciality was in formal wear and I managed an alterations sewing room for a large bridal company. The stress from the job was causing a lot of mental health issues and effecting my relationships and friendships. I found myself looking into bootcamps recommended by pals in the field. Decided to take W4B through Bitwise and won't be looking back.

Interests

I love professional wrestling and co-host a podcast with my friend Alicia. I'm also a musician and play bass/vox for a couple of bands here in Fresno. As I've stated before, I am a professional seamstress and can sew just about anything if given enough time. I am a lot older than I look.
